Healthy Foods

Incorporating fresh and nutritious ingredients is essential for a balanced diet.

Fresh Fruits & Vegetables

  • Leafy greens like spinach, kale, and collard greens
  • Fruits like berries, oranges, and bananas
  • Root vegetables such as sweet potatoes and carrots

Whole Grains

  • Brown rice
  • Oats
  • Whole wheat bread

Lean Proteins

  • Chicken breast
  • Tofu
  • Fish like salmon and trout
